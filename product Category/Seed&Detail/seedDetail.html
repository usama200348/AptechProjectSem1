Dill Seeds

Price: $12.95

Code:fine quality dill seeds

1
Description :-

To the ancient Egyptians, a soothing medicine and even used in magic spells! This versatile herb will add to many culinary delights. Seeds are popular in pickles. Position Full sun. Sow Direct into the garden. Cover with Yates Seed Raising Mix, firm down and keep moist. Thin later to 20cm apart. Care Grow in full sun but protected from the wind. Good drainage is essential. Apply Yates Thrive Soluble Fertiliser every 4 weeks. Use leaves finely chopped in soups, salads, egg, fish and meat dishes. Seeds can be used the same as the leaves, and also in cakes and bread. If left to flower, Dill will attract many garden-friendly insects that control aphids.
